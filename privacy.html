Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-29

GameOfJones schedules and posts fantasy-football update videos to a creator-owned TikTok account using TikTok’s Content Posting API.

Data We Store

  • open_id and OAuth access token for the connected TikTok account (encrypted at rest).
  • Basic profile fields from user.info.basic (handle, avatar URL) for our internal dashboard.
  • Rendered video files and captions generated by the owner.

Data We Do Not Store

  • Followers, messages, comments, or analytics from TikTok.
  • Personal data from third parties.

Use of Data

Used solely to authenticate posting and display the connected account. We do not sell or share your data.

Retention & Deletion

We retain tokens until you revoke access or request deletion. Email GameOfJones100@gmail.com to request deletion; we remove tokens, cached profile data, and unposted media within 30 days.

Security

HTTPS in transit; secrets encrypted at rest. Access limited to the owner/operator.
